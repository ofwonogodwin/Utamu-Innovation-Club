01

Our Commitment

The UTAMU Innovation Club is dedicated to building an inclusive, welcoming, and empowering community where every student—regardless of skill level, background, gender, disability, or campus location—can learn, collaborate, and innovate.

We are committed to creating a safe environment that encourages respect, growth, and equal participation.

02

Expected Behavior

All community members, including organizers, speakers, and participants, are expected to:

Respect Everyone

  • Treat all members with kindness and professionalism
  • Listen actively and allow others to express their ideas without interruption

Promote Inclusion

  • Use welcoming language that is beginner-friendly and free from harmful stereotypes
  • Encourage participation from quieter members and support those new to tech

Support Learning

  • Be patient with beginners and explain concepts clearly
  • Celebrate curiosity and encourage questions at all levels

Maintain a Safe Environment

  • Follow event rules and organizer instructions
  • Speak up if you observe behavior that violates this Code

03

Unacceptable Behavior

The following actions are strictly prohibited:

Harassment or Discrimination

  • Sexist, racist, or derogatory comments
  • Microaggressions such as interrupting or dismissing certain groups
  • Intimidation, bullying, or any form of hostility

Accessibility Violations

  • Ignoring or mocking individuals with disabilities
  • Creating environments that purposely exclude certain groups

Exclusionary Conduct

  • Making fun of beginners for asking basic questions
  • Using overly complex or gatekeeping language with the intention of belittling others

Disruptive Actions

  • Spamming, shouting down speakers, or derailing discussions
  • Sharing harmful, violent, or inappropriate content

04

Reporting an Issue

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it immediately.

05

How We Will Respond

When a violation is reported, the organizers will:

1

Acknowledge

Acknowledge the report and gather necessary information

2

Evaluate

Evaluate the severity of the incident

3

Take Action

Take appropriate action, which may include:

  • A private warning
  • Removal from a specific event
  • Temporary suspension from club activities
  • Permanent removal for severe or repeated violations

The community lead and core organizers will ensure decisions are fair, transparent, and aligned with our values.

06

Commitment to Improvement

We recognize that inclusion is a continuous process. UTAMU Innovation Club will regularly review and update this Code of Conduct to ensure it remains relevant, effective, and aligned with the needs of all members.
